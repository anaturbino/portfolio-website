Claimed is a resource for content creators to find publications using their content without associating it to their name. Using Google Lens to reverse image search materials and easily allowing users to add those unclaimed publications to their portfolios, Claimed aims to unite creators and give them the platform to maximize their portfolio while networking with people on the same field.
