Hi, I'm Ana :)

I am a UI Designer, bartender and mixologist based in Jersey City, NJ. My background starts in the hospitality industry where I have played many different roles from server to bar manager along the 8 years I have been in the industry. Additionally, I was in the nonprofit industry for 4 years, filling the roles of project coordinator, team manager, operations specialist and financial literacy instructor for a free class that took place at New Jersey City University.

Combining my background from all the positions I have held, I am well equipped with various skills such as team leading, conducting research, flexibility, problem-solving, ability to multitask and work under pressure, reporting of losses/gains, customer service, etc.

Designing has always been a passion of mine. I briefly pursued interior design and when I learned out user interface design, I knew I had to make the switch. Being able to create beautiful and useful interfaces while problem solving has been extremely fulfilling and accomplishing, and I hope to keep developing my skillset to become a valuable contributer to the UI design community and my future team.
